# Engineering Glossary

## Principal
Any actor that can request work, receive permissions, publish events, or appear in audit records.

## Resource
Anything Parker can read, write, execute, observe, protect, delete, export, or control.

## Permission
A decision about whether a Principal may perform an Action against a Resource under a Context.

## ExecutionRequest
The canonical object representing proposed work before execution.

## ExecutionResult
The structured outcome of an ExecutionRequest.

## Session
A bounded interaction context linking requests, user activity, authentication state, and conversation state.

## Event
A structured fact published by a Parker service.

## AuditRecord
An immutable record explaining a significant decision, access, or execution outcome.

## Action
A requested operation against a Resource.

## Execution
The act of carrying out approved work through the Execution Pipeline.

## Workflow
A multi-step structured process.

## Task
A tracked unit of work that may be immediate, deferred, long-running, or cancellable.
